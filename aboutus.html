Who are Kincora?


Kincora has built a leading position in the global aircraft leasing industry. The scale of our business reflects the ambition of our team, multi-cycle experience and our focus on maintaining a fleet of young, fuel-efficient aircraft. Our success is also built on clear corporate values which inform our interaction with all of our stakeholders. Our values ensure we continue to build strong relationships with all our stakeholders; maintain an attractive and inclusive environment in which to work; and invest back in our local community.
